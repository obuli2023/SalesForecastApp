Hello User!

  • This is a Sales Forecasting Application used to predict the future sales effectively!

  • This application is simple to use.

    • Follow the below steps to get your Sales Prediction Instantly:
  1. If new user, click register in the navigation bar to register,After Registration you will be navigated to user page.

  2. Existing User? Then Login with your credentials. After Login you will be directed to Dashboard.

  3. Get you Sales Prediction with just 4 inputs:

    1. Enter The date from which you have to predict

    2. Enter the deadline to which you have to predict

    3. Select the Periodicity(week,month,year)

    4. Finally upload the data set of the previous sales information

    5. Click submit to get the Prediction!
